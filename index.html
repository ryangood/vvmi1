Our Vision

Vernacular Video Mission International partners with local churches and other mission organizations who develop indigenous ministries that communicate the Gospel through multimedia in ethnic cultural communities (vernacular groups).

Our goal is to bring people into a relationship with Jesus Christ, making an eternal difference in the lives of people of many cultures where God leads us throughout the world.

We seek, through the power of God’s Word and the Holy Spirit, to energize individuals and churches for missions by personally connecting them with life changing mission opportunities. We believe that mission awareness and enthusiasm for sharing Christ grows in the local church as a result of this personal mission partnership.

We also seek to connect unreached peoples to Jesus through partnerships with existing multimedia ministries, local churches in the United States, churches in other nations, and other mission agencies.

VVMI provides mission leadership and coordinates strategic projects, short-term mission trips, and training of ethnic Christians for the promotion of vernacular evangelism and discipleship.
